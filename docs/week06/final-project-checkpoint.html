Final Project Checkpoint

The final project is due on the last day of the course which is in the middle of week 7.

Overview

The final project is an opportunity for you to demonstrate your mastery of the JavaScript language learning outcomes as presented in the course. The purpose of this checkpoint assignment if for you to stay on task with finishing your final project, ideally well before the last day of the course.

Instructions

  1. Review the Final Project Description and note the functional specifications and feature requirements.
  2. Be prepared to answer the following reflective questions:
    • What success have you noted and are most proud of during the design and development of your project?
    • What challenges have you run into during the development?
    • Name two (2) to three (3) resources that you have found useful during the the course to complete tasks like this project. Why have they been especially useful to you?
